The Fiduciary Advantage: Why Securities Licenses Matter

There is a fundamental difference between a standard insurance agent and a fully licensed Financial Fiduciary. Many advisors hold basic licenses that limit them to selling specialized products under a loose "suitability" standard.

I hold comprehensive securities licenses and operate as an Investment Advisor Representative through Infinity Financial Services, an SEC Registered Investment Advisor. This means I am legally bound to the fiduciary standard—required by federal law to place your interests above my own, completely free from hidden commissions or product-driven quotas.

From complex market equities to advanced estate coordination, my licensing allows me to build and manage a truly comprehensive, unrestricted financial architecture for your wealth.
