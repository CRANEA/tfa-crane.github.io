Career Background & Approach

My Approach: My work has centered on giving clients a more informed, structured way to evaluate important financial decisions. I approach planning as an educational process first, helping people understand their options, the tradeoffs involved, and the long-term impact of each strategy.

How I Work: I believe financial advice should be understandable, personalized, and aligned with a client’s broader objectives rather than driven by one-size-fits-all recommendations. My role is to bring fiduciary discipline, clear communication, and practical structure to complex planning conversations.

Who I Serve: I work with individuals, families, executives, and business owners who want thoughtful guidance around retirement income, wealth preservation, estate planning coordination, and tax-aware financial decision-making.
